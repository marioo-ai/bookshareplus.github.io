The problem

70 percent

of classroom materials are inaccessible to students with disabilities

Teacher-created materials are used widely in the classroom. Over two-thirds of educators use their own content to supplement or replace textbooks. Most of these materials - worksheets, PDFs, STEM content — are produced in formats that students with disabilities cannot access, preventing them from learning and participating in their education.

The Bookshare+ solution

Building on Benetech's trusted Bookshare platform, Bookshare+ extends accessibility to teacher-created content. Our AI technology transforms teacher-created content into accessible, engaging formats that work for every learner in a fraction of the time that it would take for educators to remediate it themselves.
